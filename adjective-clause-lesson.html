STEP
觀念鋪墊
一個句子一定要有主詞＋動詞
這是英文句子最基本的骨架，今天後面判斷「能不能省略」都靠這個觀念。
唯一的例外：祈使句（命令句），主詞 You 會暫時消失。
(You) Close the door. ／ (You) Be quiet.
伏筆：等一下形容詞子句裡「缺主詞」還是「缺受詞」，會決定能不能把關係詞拿掉！

3
STEP
子句總覽
英文有三大子句
名詞子句：當名詞用，可以當主詞／受詞／補語
例：What he said surprised everyone.
形容詞子句（今天主角）：修飾名詞
例：I know a girl who plays the piano well.
副詞子句：修飾整句話（原因、時間、條件…）
例：She cried because she was sad.
STEP 3 · 子句功能
7
1-3 教學示範例句
判斷句中的子句類型
簡單
① What he said surprised everyone.（名詞子句，當主詞）
② I know a girl who plays the piano well.（形容詞子句，修飾girl）
③ She cried because she was sad.（副詞子句）
學測頂標
① Whoever arrives first will get the best seat, which is placed near the stage.
② The reason why he quit his job remains unclear even though many people have asked him.
STEP 3 · 子句功能
8
1-3 學生練習
判斷底線是哪種子句
簡單
1. I don't know what time it is.看答案名詞子句（當know的受詞）
2. The book that is on the table belongs to me.看答案形容詞子句（修飾book）
3. He left early because he felt sick.看答案副詞子句
學測頂標
1. Whether she will come or not depends on the weather, which nobody can predict.看答案Whether=名詞子句(主詞)；which=形容詞子句(非限定)
2. The city where I was born has changed a lot since I moved away.看答案where=形容詞子句；since=副詞子句
STEP 3 · 子句功能

4
STEP
今天的主角登場
形容詞子句要放在名詞後面
中文習慣：修飾語放前面 ——「昨天買的書」
英文習慣：修飾語太長（一整句話）要放後面
中：我昨天買的那本書弄丟了。
英：The book that I bought yesterday is lost.
結論：修飾名詞的一整句話，要用一個連接詞接在名詞後面——這個連接詞就是今天的主角。

STEP
複習 → 銜接新觀念
關係代名詞 ＝代名詞 ＋ 連接詞
複習：代名詞是用來代替一個名詞，避免重複。
He is my brother. He is a doctor. → 不想重複 He...
關係代名詞多了一個功能：除了「代替名詞」，還能同時當連接詞，把兩句話合成一句！
接下來我們就用「拆句 → 合句」的練習，實際感受這個雙重功能。
STEP 5 · 關係代名詞
11
拆句合句 教學示範
先找相同的名詞，再用 that 連接
簡單
① I have a friend. + The friend lives in Taipei. → I have a friend that lives in Taipei.
② She bought a car. + The car is red. → She bought a car that is red.
③ He is reading a book. + I gave him the book. → He is reading a book that I gave him.
學測頂標
① The scientist published a paper that challenged a theory that had been accepted for decades.（雙層形容詞子句）
② Many students admire the professor whose research has influenced policies worldwide.（用that會卡住，需改用whose）
STEP 5 · 拆句合句
12
2-2 學生練習
合併成一句
簡單
1. I met a man. + The man works at a hospital.看答案I met a man that works at a hospital.
2. This is the movie. + Everyone is talking about the movie.看答案This is the movie that everyone is talking about.
3. She wrote a letter. + The letter made her mother cry.看答案She wrote a letter that made her mother cry.
學測頂標
1. The company launched a product. + The product received criticism that no one had expected.看答案The company launched a product that received criticism that no one had expected.
2. The novel describes a war. + The war changed the course of history.看答案The novel describes a war that changed the course of history.

3-1 核心判斷力
關代代替主詞還是受詞？
口訣：看先行詞原本在子句裡的位置——動作是他發出的（主詞），還是他承受的（受詞）？

STEP 6 · 主詞受詞判斷
14
3-1 學生練習
判斷底線關代是主詞還是受詞
簡單
1. The man who called you is my uncle.看答案主詞
2. The cake that she made was delicious.看答案受詞
3. Students who study hard usually get good grades.看答案主詞
學測頂標
1. The report which the journalist wrote in a hurry contained several factual errors.看答案受詞
2. The employee who had been warned twice was finally dismissed.看答案主詞
STEP 6 · 主詞受詞判斷
15
3-2 關係代名詞家族
who / whom / whose / which / that
先行詞當主詞當受詞表所有格
人who / thatwho(m) / thatwhose
物which / thatwhich / thatwhose（of which）
that 幾乎萬用，但也有「不能用」跟「一定要用」的場合——留到 STEP 8 細講。

STEP
4-1 省略判斷
受詞可省，主詞不可
回到STEP1的伏筆：受詞省略後子句仍有主詞+動詞，結構完整；主詞省略後子句會沒有主詞，動詞失去依靠。
簡單
① The book (that) I bought is interesting.（受詞，可省）
② The man who called you is my teacher.（主詞，不可省）
學測頂標
① The plan (that) the manager proposed at the meeting was rejected by the board.（受詞可省，中間插入片語）
STEP 7 · 省略與化簡
20
4-1 學生練習
能不能省略？可以的話請改寫
簡單
1. The movie (that) I watched last night was boring.看答案可省：The movie I watched last night was boring.
2. The girl who won the race is my classmate.看答案不可省（主詞）
3. The letter (that) he wrote never arrived.看答案可省：The letter he wrote never arrived.
學測頂標
1. The article (that) the professor recommended sparked a heated debate in class.看答案可省：The article the professor recommended sparked a heated debate in class.
2. The witness who testified in court received death threats afterward.看答案不可省（主詞）
STEP 7 · 省略與化簡
21
4-2 分詞片語化簡
主詞省略時，動詞要改成分詞
30秒複習：先行詞是動作發出者→主動→Ving；先行詞是動作承受者→被動→Vpp。
簡單（有be動詞 vs 無be動詞）
① The man who is standing there... → The man standing there...（有be動詞，直接刪）
② The letter which was written by him... → The letter written by him...（留Vpp）
③ The train which leaves at 6... → The train leaving at 6...（無be動詞，主動改Ving）
STEP 7 · 省略與化簡
22
4-2 教學示範（學測頂標）
同句可能同時出現 Ving 和 Vpp
學測頂標
① The proposal which was submitted by the intern... → The proposal submitted by the intern...（Vpp被動）
② Passengers who arrive late... the flight departing at noon. → Passengers arriving late... the flight departing at noon.（兩個Ving，需分別判斷）
學生練習·簡單
1. The boy who is playing basketball is my brother.看答案The boy playing basketball is my brother.（主動Ving）
2. The window that was broken by the storm needs repair.看答案The window broken by the storm needs repair.（被動Vpp）
3. The students who study in the library are preparing for exams.看答案The students studying in the library...（主動Ving）
STEP 7 · 省略與化簡
23
4-2 學生練習（學測頂標）+ 總覽表
省略 vs Ving vs Vpp 總覽
學測頂標
1. The report which was written in a rush contained several errors that were later corrected by the editor.看答案...written in a rush contained several errors later corrected by the editor.（皆為Vpp）
2. Employees who fail to meet the targets set by the manager will be reviewed.看答案Employees failing to meet the targets set by the manager...（failing主動；set被動）
情況條件範例
直接省略關代關代當受詞The book (that) I bought...
改Ving關代當主詞・無be動詞・主動the train leaving at 6
改Vpp關代當主詞・無be動詞・被動the report written by him
只留分詞關代當主詞・有be動詞the man (who is) standing there

STEP
5-1 that的特殊脾氣
什麼時候一定要用 that？
信號字：the only／the very／all／no／序數詞／最高級／all-類代名詞（everything, nothing...）
簡單
① This is the only book that can solve your problem.
② He is the most interesting person that I have ever met.（最高級）
③ I have read everything that he wrote.
學測頂標
① The very reason that she resigned has never been made public.
STEP 8 · that的限制
25
5-1 學生練習
判斷是否必須用 that
簡單
1. He is the tallest student ___ I have ever taught. (A) that (B) who看答案A（最高級信號）
2. I like everything ___ she cooks. (A) which (B) that看答案B
3. The only person ___ can help you is Mr. Lin. (A) who (B) that看答案B
學測頂標
1. The first book that made him famous and the movie that followed were both huge successes. (A) which...which (B) that...that看答案B（序數詞the first信號）
2. The only student and his dog ___ appeared in the photo were both awarded. (A) who (B) that看答案B（人+物同時出現）
STEP 8 · that的限制
26
9
STEP
5-2 一個逗號的差別
限定用法 vs 非限定用法
簡單
① I have two brothers who are teachers.（限定，可能還有其他兄弟）
② I have two brothers, who are teachers.（非限定，只有兩個兄弟）
非限定規則：要逗號・不能用that・關代不能省略・可用which指代整件事
學測頂標
① He failed the final exam, which disappointed his parents deeply.（which指代整件事）
STEP 9 · 限定非限定
27
5-2 學生練習
判斷限定／非限定，選出正確關係詞
簡單
1. The book ___ is on the desk belongs to me. (A) that (B) , which,看答案A（限定）
2. My brother, ___ lives in the U.S., is coming home. (A) that (B) who看答案B（非限定不能用that）
3. Students ___ arrive late will not be admitted. (A) who (B) , who,看答案A（限定）
學測頂標
1. She won the scholarship, ___ came as a huge surprise to her family. (A) that (B) which看答案B（which指代整件事）
2. The novel, ___ was translated into 20 languages, brought fame ___ he had never expected. (A) which...that (B) that...which看答案A（第一格非限定用which，第二格限定用that）

STEP 10 · 總結
今天的易混淆總表
判斷點規則
先行詞是人還是物who/whom/whose（人）vs which（物），that皆可
主詞還是受詞主詞不可直接省略；受詞可整個省略
有無be動詞有be動詞可直接刪掉「關代+be」
主動還是被動主動改Ving；被動改Vpp
特殊信號字the only/all/最高級/序數詞/all-類代名詞 → 必須用that
有無逗號有逗號＝非限定，不能用that，關代不能省略
總結 · 易混淆總表
29
綜合練習 · 簡單
混合以上所有規則
1. The woman ___ is talking to my mom is my aunt. (A) who (B) that看答案兩者皆可（主詞用法）
2. The gift (that) my friend gave me was a book.看答案受詞可省
3. The car ___ was parked outside got towed. (A) parking (B) parked看答案B（被動Vpp）
先套用上一頁的總表，一步一步判斷，不要憑語感亂猜。

綜合練習 · 學測頂標
挑戰題：一句多個判斷點
1. The manager, ___ proposal was rejected twice, finally resigned, ___ shocked everyone. (A) whose...which (B) who...that看答案A（所有格whose；非限定指代整件事which）
2. Among the candidates ___ applied for the position, the only one ___ had prior experience was hired immediately, a decision ___ surprised no one.
這題較複雜，建議當堂用「討論」帶過：讓學生一步步套用總表判斷三個空格，而不是直接公布答案。
